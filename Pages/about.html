Overview

Crystals Lodge is a new establishment located in Kalimbuka area, Zomba, Malawi, behind the mental hospital. Surrounded by the natural beauty of the region, Crystals Lodge offers a unique and tranquil retreat for both leisure and business travelers.

Our lodge offers a range of services including accommodation, dining facilities, and conference amenities. Our rooms are equipped with modern conveniences such as a mini-fridge, air conditioners, jacuzzi, Smart TV, DSTV, Wi-Fi, and more.

At Crystals Lodge, we are committed to providing high-quality services. Our team comprises dedicated professionals focused on delivering exceptional results and ensuring customer satisfaction.

At Crystals Lodge, the safety and security of our guests are paramount. Our commitment to your safety is reflected in the comprehensive security measures we have implemented:Brick Fenced Perimeter,Electric Wire Fencing ,24-Hour Guard Services ,State-of-the-Art Alarm Systems , Emergency Response

Our mission is to create a welcoming and rejuvenating environment for our guests, offering exceptional service, comfortable accommodations, and a range of amenities that showcase the beauty of Zomba and its surroundings.

To be the preferred destination that seamlessly blends modern comfort with the enchanting allure of Zomba's natural landscape, providing guests with an unforgettable experience
