our offers

accomodation

Our lodge features a collection of well-appointed rooms and suites designed to provide a perfect blend of modern amenities and luxurious sophistication. Each room is tastefully decorated, offering a cozy and relaxing atmosphere for our guests.

Indulge in the ultimate relaxation experience with amenities such as jacuzzis, queen-size beds, unique TV stands with integrated fridges, lounge areas, air conditioning, hair dryers, and more. Whether you're traveling for business or leisure, our rooms offer a tranquil retreat to unwind and rejuvenate after a day of exploration or meetings.

events and conference

Crystals Lodge is equipped with state-of-the-art conference facilities, making it an ideal venue for corporate meetings, conferences, and special events.

We understand that every event is unique, and we are committed to making your conference a memorable success.Our venue offers state-of-the-art facilities designed to host events of all sizes, from intimate seminars to large-scale conferences. We pride ourselves on providing an environment that fosters creativity, collaboration, and learning.

restaurant

Step into our restaurant and embark on a culinary journey that delights the senses and tantalizes the taste buds. Nestled within the heart of Crystals Lodge, our restaurant offers a warm and inviting ambiance, where every meal is an experience to savor.

Savor a diverse array of delectable dishes crafted by our talented chefs using the freshest locally-sourced ingredients. From gourmet delights to traditional favorites, our menu caters to every palate and preference. Whether you're craving international cuisine or local delicacies, our restaurant promises a culinary adventure that exceeds expectations
